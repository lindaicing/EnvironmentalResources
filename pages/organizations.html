Organizations

  • OPEC - Organization of the Petroleum Exporting Countries. Intergovernmental org made up of 13 countries, has a major influence on global oil prices.

US Organizations

  • Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) - US Governmental independent executive agency, created to protect the environment. Established by Richard Nixon on December 2, 1970. Originally proposed by Nixon on July 9, 1970.

International Organizations

  • IPCC - Intergovernmental Panel on Climate Change. Body of the united nations that analyzes the political, economic, and social impacts of human-induced climate change. Founded in 1988.

Key Conferences

  • COP - Conference of the Parties. COP26 is the most recent conference. The Parties (197 nations part of the UNFCCC) met in Glasgow (2021) to discuss agreements on tackling climate change.

Activist Organizations

  • Sierra Club - Grassroots environmental US Organization
