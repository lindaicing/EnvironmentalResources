General Terms

  • Aerosol - suspension of fine particles in air or another gas
  • Agroecosystem - ecosystem within an area of agricultural activity
  • Agroforestry - intentional planting of trees/shrubs around crops to enhance functionality and sustainability of farming system
  • Agromining - growing hyperaccumulator plants as a crop, then processing it to extract heavy metals
    • Hyperaccumulator Plants (Metallophytes) - plants that can tolerate high levels of heavy metals such as lead
  • Biomimicry - Designs based on biological structures
    • Ex. Human engineered antibacterial nano-coating based off of the microscopic wing structure of dragonflies
  • Biodiversity - Biological variety of organisms on earth (or a particular ecosystem)
  • Biotechnology - Using living systems to make products
  • Ecological Succession - How an ecological community changes over time
  • Ecosystem - All organisms in an area, and how they interact with each other and their environment
  • Paleoclimate - The climate during a certain time period
