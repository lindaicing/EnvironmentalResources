General Terms

  • Aerosol - suspension of fine particles in air or another gas
  • Agroecosystem - ecosystem within an area of agricultural activity
  • Agroforestry - intentional planting of trees/shrubs around crops to enhance functionality and sustainability of farming system
  • Agromining - growing hyperaccumulator plants as a crop, then processing it to extract heavy metals
    • Hyperaccumulator Plants (Metallophytes) - plants that can tolerate high levels of heavy metals such as lead
