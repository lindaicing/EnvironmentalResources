Environmental Resources

What is this?

This is a resource for people who want to learn more about how to be more environmentally conscious in their everyday lives. It includes definitions for environmentally-related terms, important legislation, and interesting readings and videos. The goal is to add critiques of all the listed resources, since there's a lot of cool stuff out there and even more to learn.

Non-Amazon links are provided for both book purchase links. If possible, check out if a local bookstore or community libraries have copies. Please stay safe and healthy!
